Hi, my name is Adam El-Naggar. Thanks for stopping by.

Breathe is a simple meditation timer I created. I wanted to make a simple timer for my own use that was inspiring and aesthetically pleasing. I hope I’ve achieved that and some of you find value in it as well.

Meditation is an opportunity to quieten down the world for a few moments, breathe, and allow yourself to just be for a while. It can have all sorts of benefits and I believe it is the essence of personal maintenance. If you want to try but are unsure what to do, I recommend reading this short article from zen habits, titled Meditation for Beginners.

Pressing ‘Inspire’ will serve you with a random quote that has resonated with me, from a list I’ve compiled. Some of them are particularly relevant to meditation and some aren’t, but all of them were chosen to encourage reflection.

If you want to give me any feedback or suggestions (which are very welcome and much appreciated), or just want to chat, connect with me on Twitter. Here's my website if you want to see some other things I've done.

And don't forget to breathe.
